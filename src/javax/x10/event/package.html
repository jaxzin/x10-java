Provides interfaces and classes for dealing with different types of events fired by X10 components. {@link javax.x10.X10Receiver}s are the major producer of {@link javax.x10.event.X10Event}s, and {@link javax.x10.X10Transmitter}s are the only producer of {@link javax.x10.event.X10TransmitterEvent}s. {@link javax.x10.X10Transmitter}s can also accept user created {@link javax.x10.event.X10Event}s and deliver them through some implementation-specific media.
